About This Project

Managing personal finances is an important skill for every student. Many students struggle to keep track of their daily expenses, which can lead to overspending and financial stress. The Student Finance Tracker was developed to provide a simple and effective solution that helps students monitor their spending habits, organize financial records, and make informed decisions about their money.

This application allows users to record transactions, categorize expenses, set spending limits, and analyze financial trends over time. By maintaining accurate financial records, students can better understand where their money is going and identify areas where they can reduce unnecessary expenses.

The app is designed with usability, accessibility, and responsiveness in mind. Whether accessed on a mobile phone, tablet, or desktop, it provides a consistent and user-friendly experience. It runs entirely in the browser with no account, no server, and no internet connection required after the first load.

Key Features

Everything you need to stay on top of your spending

  • Spending Dashboard:Real-time total, top category, 7-day trend chart, and budget tracker at a glance.
  • 5-Rule Regex Validation:Every input guarded by regex, including a back-reference rule that catches duplicate words.
  • Live Regex Search:Filter any field with full regex support. Matches highlight instantly; bad patterns show clear feedback.
  • Multi-Currency:Work in USD, RWF, or UGX with configurable exchange rates, switchable anytime in Settings.
  • Auto-Save & Export:Changes save to localStorage instantly. Export as JSON or import from a backup anytime.
  • Fully Accessible:Semantic HTML, ARIA live regions, keyboard shortcuts, skip link, and 4.5:1 colour contrast.

Dashboard Overview

The Dashboard serves as the central hub of the Student Finance Tracker. It provides a comprehensive summary of financial activities including total records, total spending, top category, and a 7-day trend chart. Users can also set a monthly budget cap and the system will alert them when spending exceeds the limit.

Financial Records & Expense Management

The Records section allows users to create, view, edit, and manage all transaction data. Every record contains a description, amount, category, and date, each assigned a unique ID with created/updated timestamps. Advanced regex search provides instant visual highlighting of matched results.

How to Get Started

  1. Open the Dashboard:Click “Open Dashboard” above. Sample transactions load automatically on your first visit.
  2. Add a transaction:Go to Add Record, fill in the description, amount, category, and date, then save.
  3. Set a budget cap:Go to Settings and enter a monthly spending limit.
  4. Search and filter:Switch to Records and type any keyword or regex pattern into the search box.
  5. Export your data:Use the Export button on the dashboard to download a JSON backup.
